Itinerary
  • Airport to Sigiriya

    SIGIRIYA is an historical city which has a deep historical value. It is situated in the north central province of Sri Lanka. It is not only a rock but also a fortress used to be protective from invaders. It was built by king Kashyapa. There’s a nice and an interesting story also which was written around that place. Once it was nominated as a world's heritage.

    Things to see on the way & on the day
    • Pinnavala elephant orphanage 1/30 - 2 Hours Entrance Fee
    • Hiking to Sigiriya rock to see the sunset 2 - 2/30 Hours Entrance Fee
  • Sigiriya to Kandy

    KANDY is a cultural city and very famous as a main city in Sri Lanka. It belongs to the central province of Sri Lanka. And it can be known as a sacred city with the world famous lord Buddha’s sacred tooth relic.

    Things to see on the way & on the day
    • Dambulla cave temple 1/30 - 2 Hours Entrance Fee
    • Nalanda gedige 30 Minutes
    • Matale Hindu kovil 30 Minutes Entrance Fee
    • Evening cultural dancing show at Kandy 1 - 1/30 Hours Entrance Fee
    • Ceremony at Kandy tooth temple 1 Hours
  • Kandy to Ella

    ELLA is very touristic with its own nature with mist and cold, Flowers blossom in the sunshine make the nature very beauty. It is very famous to do hiking and for beautiful accommodations and tasty food.

    Things to see on the way & on the day
    • Morning at Royal botanical garden in Peradeniya 1/30 - 2 Hours Entrance Fee
    • Ramboda Water falls 45 Minutes
    • Tea factories 30 Minutes
    • Tea plantations 30 Minutes
    • Nuwaraeliya city tour (Ancient post office, Local market ,Gregory lake and park) 10 Minutes
  • Ella to Bentota

    BENTOTA is an area with a nice beach view. And the area surrounded with famous madu river, It is well famous for water sports , Turtle conservation project takes an important place in the area.

    Things to see on the way & on the day
    • Ravana waterfall 30 Minutes
    • Buduruwagala temple 30 Minutes Entrance Fee
    • Udawalawa elephant orphanage 1 Hour Entrance Fee
    • Galle fort 1 - 1/30 Hours
  • Bentota to Airport via Colombo

    COLOMBO is the main city in Sri Lanka and its crowdie city with lots of people and service suppliers. It belongs to western province of the country. It has become a main touristic point not because of its beauty but because of the services can obtain there.

    Things to see on the way & on the day
    • Gangaramaya temple 45 Minutes Entrance Fee
    • Independence square 30 Minutes
    • Galle face 30 Minutes
    • Colombo fort 15 Minutes
    • National museum 30 Minutes Entrance Fee
    • Outside visit of Old parliament
